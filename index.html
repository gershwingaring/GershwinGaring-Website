My passion for acting did not start until my freshmen year in highschool. Growing up I wanted to be singer. Nevertheless; when I first joined my acting class with my teacher, Ms. Wong, that was when I knew that this is the path that I want to pursue. I starred in different plays in the years of 2015 to 2019 (my freshmen year through my first year in college). Some examples of the play that I have done are "We Were Young that Year", "Flowers for Algernon", and many more. I started acting in my highschool years up to now. The first acting role I ever got was in my freshman year. We had two plays that year and I was apart of both. Luckily enough, in one of the plays I played as the lead actor. From freshman year to senior year I did at least one play and continued to learn and grow more from acting. I was also recruited for an acting program during the summer; I was able to showcase some of my acting and singing skills. In each different acting I learned many different skills that I have used until up to this day.Some skills that I have that would be an asset in this position are my communication skills, positive attitude, and willingness to learn. I have developed my communication skill through the clubs that I joined back in my high school years. ASB (Associated Student Body), NHS (National Honors Society), Drama Club, and ACappella are some clubs that enabled me to showcase my communication skills. Working with many individuals helped me become an active listener, not only that but I became a better leader. Walking around campus with just a simple smile on my face was my way of showing positivity.As a leader, I also learned to always have a positive attitude. Having a positive attitude helps me to become a better person, thus a better actor. Positive attitude is very much needed when it comes to acting.Being nice will help with finding diffferent connections with different people. Willingness to learn has always been a part of me. I believe these different skills helped shaped the profession I am taking. Being an actor is not just all about acting; it is also about having a set of skills. As said above, communication skill will help me deliver my feelings or emotions when I am not understanding something. Furthermore, this will help me communicate better with future directors, writers, etc. In addition, having patience is also very helpful in the acting industry. Long hours of work and working with a large group of people will definitely test one's patience. And so, with this skill, I have learned that not only does it help with being able to keep a temper down but, it also helps to get all the job done quicker.
